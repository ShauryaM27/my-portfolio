Our Story

Founded in 2018 by music therapist Sarah Johnson and teen mental health advocate Michael Chen, M4TMH began as a small community initiative and has grown into a national movement.

We believe in the transformative power of music to heal, comfort, and empower young minds navigating the challenges of adolescence.

Our Approach

1 Music as Therapy

We create safe spaces where teens can explore emotions through curated playlists, songwriting, and music appreciation.

2 Community Building

Our peer-led discussion groups help teens connect over shared musical interests and experiences.

3 Professional Support

Licensed therapists are available to guide teens when deeper support is needed.
